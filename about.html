College Life

I started at Moravian University in Fall of 2022 while working part time as a server in a hometown resturaunt. I was lucky enough to be able to dorm with two of my good friends from highschool, not lucky enought to get a dorm with air conditioning.
My friend took intro to comp sci his first semester while I rushed to get my general education classes done. By the time the second semester was coming up, I had no clue what I wanted to pursue. After my friend showed me some of his projects I thought, why not try comp sci?
So, I did, and what a ride it's been since. I'm now entering my third year as a computer science major and I am thrilled to continue building my online presence starting here.
